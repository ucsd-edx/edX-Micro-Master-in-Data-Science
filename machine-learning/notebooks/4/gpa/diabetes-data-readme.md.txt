# Diabetes dataset

from From Bradley Efron, Trevor Hastie, Iain Johnstone and Robert Tibshirani (2004) "Least Angle Regression," Annals of Statistics (with discussion), 407-499, we have

442 diabetes patients

Ten baseline variables:
> age, sex, body mass index, average blood pressure, and six blood serum measurements 
One response variable: 
> quantitative measure of disease progression one year after baseline
